### Characteristic Failure Modes

The substantive engagement with the characteristic failure modes of each scaling mechanism is constitutive of the cultivation-condition analytical engagement. Norms scale through ritual-and-prestige amplification but generate substantive exclusion and out-group hostility; the substantive cultivation-condition failure mode is the generation of substantive structural oppression along the lines of norm-prescribed inclusion. Ritual scales coordinated cooperation but generates costly-signaling-and-cult dynamics; the substantive cultivation-condition failure mode is the substantive cost imposed by ritual signaling on participants and the substantive risks of cult-like substantive engagement. Prestige scales coordination but generates substantive oligarchy and substantive concentrated cultural authority; the substantive cultivation-condition failure mode is the generation of substantive concentrated power along lines of prestige accumulation, engaged at substantive depth in Documents B and C. Fictive kinship scales solidarity but generates substantive ethnic-and-national exclusion; the substantive cultivation-condition failure mode is the generation of substantive in-group/out-group political dynamics. Imagined communities scale political coordination but generate substantive nationalism and substantive political-violence pathologies the comparative-nationalism literature engages.

The substantive analytical commitment under the fifth-pass framing is that the cultural-evolutionary scaling mechanisms are the substantive cultivation conditions under which the biocultural polity has historically scaled, with their characteristic failure modes constituting the substantive scale-mismatch problems the rest of the arc engages.
