# The Political-Economic-Disturbance Diagnostic: Currency, Supply Chain, Debt, Labor Displacement, Capital Concentration

## Opening — The Document Under the Fifth-Pass Architecture

This document is the Part II political-economic-disturbance regime diagnostic. The project rests on a load-bearing triad: biocultural governance as constitutional ontology, eco-mimetic policy-making as operational method, disturbance-adapted implementation as temporal orientation. The Indigenous and Eastern traditions are primary articulators; the Western complex-adaptive-systems, ecology, biomimicry, and disturbance-ecology canons are recent and partial recovery.

The political-economic-disturbance regime threatens the political-economic substrate of the biocultural polity through currency-and-monetary shocks, supply-chain collapse, debt-crisis cascades, technological labor displacement, and capital concentration. Coordination with Document C (Part II — Oligarchy, Dark Money, and the Architecture of Implementation Capture) specifies the concrete capture architecture at the cultural-political-economic intersection; Document W (Part II — Disturbance-Regime Interactions) specifies the broader cross-regime architecture.

## Part Six — Cross-Regime Interactions Coordination with Documents C and W

The substantive cross-regime interactions operate across multiple couplings. The **political-economic-cultural coupling** operates through substantive capital concentration reproducing caste and oligarchy across generations — substantively in Document C's capture architecture; substantive technological labor displacement reproducing substantive racial and embodied inequality. The **political-economic-cognitive coupling** operates through substantive surveillance capitalism as substantive concentrated political-economic-and-cognitive disturbance; substantive algorithmic labor-market discrimination; substantive AI-driven capital concentration. The **political-economic-biological coupling** operates through substantive pandemic-and-supply-chain coupling exemplified by COVID-19; substantive political economy of pharmaceutical development; substantive food-system-and-pandemic coupling. The **political-economic-physical-environmental coupling** operates through substantive climate-and-financialization literature and substantive fossil-fuel-political-economy archive.

Document C carries the substantive concrete capture architecture at the cultural-political-economic intersection; Document W specifies the substantive broader cross-regime architecture; Document V specifies the substantive political-economic-regime side of each coupling.
